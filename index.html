Impact Token Economy

Blockchain-Powered Social Impact Tokenization (SIT)

About the Concept

Impact Token Economy is a Social Impact Tokenization (SIT) framework that rewards communities with digital Impact Tokens for achieving measurable social goals such as cleanliness, sustainability, public health, and civic participation.

How It Works

  1. Define measurable social benchmarks.
  2. Verify achievements through audits or digital monitoring.
  3. Mint Impact Tokens upon milestone completion.
  4. Redeem tokens for tax rebates, subsidies, or essential goods.

Key Benefits

  • Transparent blockchain-based verification
  • Accountable public incentive structure
  • Encourages community participation
  • Scalable for villages, cities, and national programs

Vision

To build a decentralized, transparent, and performance-driven civic ecosystem where communities are rewarded for measurable social progress.
